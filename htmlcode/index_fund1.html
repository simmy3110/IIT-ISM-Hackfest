Project:
IOT Based Automatic Vehicle Accident Detection and Rescue System

This proposed IOT based accident detection system helps to reduce the loss of life due to accidents and also reduces the time taken by the ambulance to reach the hospital. To detect the accident there is accelerometer sensor present in this rescue system and the GSM module included sends messages about the location to the respective guardian and rescue team. With the help of accelerometer sensor signal, a severe accident due to an obstacle can be recognized.

Duration of Project: 1 month
